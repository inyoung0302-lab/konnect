Korea Essentials

Everything you need before & during your trip.

📱
SIM Card / Pocket WiFi
Stay connected from day 1
1
At Incheon Airport: Head to Arrival Hall — carriers (KT, SK, LG U+) have counters. Tourist SIM from ₩15,000 for 10 days.
2
Pre-order online: Recommended — Klook or Airalo eSIM. Skip the airport queue.
3
Need WiFi? Pocket WiFi rental at airport or at Wowpass kiosks nationwide.
Order SIM via Klook →
💳
Wowpass (Transport + Money)
One card for subway, bus, shopping
1
Get a Wowpass card at kiosks near major stations (Myeongdong, Hongdae, Gangnam). ₩4,000 card fee.
2
Load foreign currency directly at the kiosk — supports USD, JPY, EUR, CNY and more. Best exchange rates.
3
Use everywhere: Subway, bus, convenience stores, restaurants. Works like T-Money.
Learn more at Wowpass →
🚇
Getting Around Seoul
Subway, bus, taxi — decoded
1
Subway is cheapest (₩1,400+). Use Kakao Map or Naver Map for directions. English signage everywhere.
2
KakaoTaxi is Uber equivalent. Download the app. You can set destination in English.
3
Airport Railroad (AREX): Incheon → Seoul Station in 43 min. ₩9,500. Runs 5:23am–midnight.
Open Naver Map →

💡 Good to Know
🌙 Convenience stores (CU, GS25, 7-eleven) are open 24/7 everywhere — they sell medicine, food, ATMs.
💳 No cash? Global ATMs at every convenience store. Citibank ATMs accept most foreign cards.
📲 Translation emergency: Type in Papago or show the phone screen to Koreans — they're very helpful.
🚕 Taxi tip: Show your Naver Map route to the driver — the destination in Korean on screen is enough.
